April 26th, 2022

Blanka and Sakura join Fortnite April 28th

Epic Games announced two new Street Fighter characters will be joining the Fortnite × Street Fighter collaboration, Blanka and Sakura Kasugano! Inside, check out Blanka and Sakura's Fortnite costumes, character renders, artwork, and videos. Fornite players can begin using Blanka and Sakura starting this Thursday, April 28th, 2022.


April 25th, 2022

Street fighter III: 3rd strike Super Meter Voice Sampled from 1996 Batman Series

Throughout the decades, fighting game fans and historians have discovered the original source material for iconic character designs, illustrations, and even audio. The latest discovery, pointed out by Fairly Frequent FG Facts on Twitter, reveals the Street Fighter III: 3rd Strike sound clip (voice), used when one meter of the super gauge filled, is in fact sampled from the 1966 Batman series starring Adam West.


April 21st, 2022

Guilty Gear Strive Another Story Arrives April 29th

Arc System Works announced today that Guilty Gear STRIVE's second story mode, dubbed Another Story, will become available on Friday, April 29th for Season Pass 1 holders. Accompanying the announcement is a new batch of screenshots showing Baiken, May, and Ramlethal — all of whom will be starring in the new story experience (as they did not appear in the original story mode)..


April 18th, 2022

Umbrella Now Available in Skullgirls: 2nd Encore

Hidden Variable Games announced Umbrella, the latest DLC character of Skullgirls: 2nd Encore, is now playable in the PlayStation 4 and Steam versions of the game. Umbrella can be purchased individually for $10 or as part of the $35 Season 1 Pass. Hidden Variable also shared a trailer for Umbrella's Season 1 color palettes and voice pack.

Boss Challenge & Omega Rugal Coming to KOF XV April 14, How to Unlock Rugal's "Omega Armor" Skin

Last month, SNK announced iconic KOF series boss Rugal Bernstein, in his "Omega" form, will be joining The King of Fighters XV as free DLC. Alongside new playable character Omega Rugal, SNK confirmed new mode "Boss Challenge" will be added to the game on Thursday April 14th, 2022. On Twitter today, SNK revealed how players can unlock Omega Rugal's "Omega Armor" skin and shared a new screenshot.
